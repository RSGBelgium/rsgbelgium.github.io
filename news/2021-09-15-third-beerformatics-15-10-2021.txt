16:T1301,<p>Are you curious what it takes to start your own business? You have a great bioinformatic project in mind but you don't know where to start? Are you asking yourself what are the challenges and hurdles that must be tackled when launching a new company?</p>
<img src="{{site.baseurl}}/assets/img/logo/beerformatics_logo.svg" style="max-width:150px;">
<p>Mark your calendar (or click <a href="%7B%7Bsite.baseurl%7D%7D/assets/ics/20211015-beerformatics.ics">here</a>)! On <strong>Friday October 15</strong> we will hold the third RSG Belgium <strong>beerformatics</strong> where you will hear from our invited speakers their experience about creating and running a <strong>bioinformatics startup</strong>.</p>
<p>You're not yet fully convinced to come? Hear that: the event is <strong>free</strong> and we <strong>offer the first drink</strong>! Spots are limited so make sure to <a href="https://bit.ly/rsg_beerformatics_3" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">register now!</a></p>
<h2>Invited startups</h2>
<h3>ML2GROW</h3>
<p><a href="https://www.ml2grow.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">ML2Grow</a> was created in May 2017 as a spin-off company of Ghent University and the world-leading R&#x26;D and innovation hub in nanoelectronics and digital technologies, imec. Decades of experience in machine learning research and industry collaborations were transformed into ML2Grow to help organisations with their increasing data-related needs. A strategic partnership was created in February 2019 with Invibes Advertising, an advanced technology company specializing in digital in-feed advertisement.</p>
<h3>BioLizard</h3>
<p><a href="https://lizard.bio/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">BioLizard</a> is an agile bioinformatics consulting company with expertise in scientific data analysis, data mining, machine learning and artificial intelligence, multi-omics data, pipeline development, and more. Using predictive analytics to analyse biological data is key to fully understanding complex biological processes and can provide vital information for major science fields such as medicine and healthcare sciences, climate sciences, physics, biotechnology, and more. BioLizard specialises in gathering a diverse range of expertise and implementing the most up-to-date technological tools to meet any data needs. They provide services globally and collaborate with many organisations across the world.</p>
